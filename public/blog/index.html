How much is your time worth?

Written By Larissa on 05/23

A little piece of information that has since me become the youngest senior manager at my firm is this. If I was paying someone else to do what I'm doing now, is it worth the cost? Understanding the value of your time and squeezing it for every little drop of value is how I have lived my life. Optimise your time, stay focused and most importantly delegate intelligently and put the right systems in place. A better system gives you exponential value and increases everyone's output.
